# Brand-market-fit scorecard

Copy-paste and fill in. One block per dimension; the funnel is scored on its **weakest
link**, not the average. Score scale: 🟢 Strong · 🟡 Adequate · 🟠 Weak · 🔴 Critical.
Recommendation severity: 🔴 blocking · 🟠 important · 🟡 nit · 🔵 suggestion · 📚 learning · 🌟 praise.

> **Rule:** every metric needs a *number*, a *source*, and a *window*. No data → write
> `n/a — proxy: <what you used>` and lower confidence on that dimension. Never invent a number.
